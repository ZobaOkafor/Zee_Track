About Zee Track

The inspiration for Zee Track came from my personal struggle with staying organized. As someone who juggles multiple projects, I needed a tool that could help me keep track of my tasks efficiently. This project began as a simple idea and evolved over time, culminating in what you see today. It is also a Portfolio Project for my ALX-SE Programme, where I have been honing my skills as a developer. You can learn more about ALX here.
